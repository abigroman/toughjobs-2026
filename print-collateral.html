The bottom line

Print marketing for trades businesses in the Midwest.

Print marketing for contractors still works because it does something digital can't — it puts a physical, branded piece in a homeowner's hand and keeps it on the fridge, in the truck, or by the phone. At Toughjobs, we design and produce trade print collateral for home-services businesses across Illinois, Iowa, Missouri, Wisconsin and Indiana, from the Quad Cities and Peoria to Dubuque and anywhere within about three hours of home. Brochures, direct mailers, door hangers, yard signs, business cards, flyers, print ads and trade-show booths — every piece built on your brand so you look like one company everywhere a customer sees you.

The magic isn't any single piece — it's consistency. When your door hanger, your truck wrap, your business card and your website all share the same logo, colors and voice, a one-truck operation starts to look like the established name in the market. That's how you earn trust before you ever knock on the door. We pair sharp design with copy that actually sells the job, then handle premium printing, finishing, and neighborhood-targeted mailing so all you do is approve the proof.

Whether you need a single door-hanger drop for this weekend or a full collateral kit that matches your rebrand, we scope it to your goals and your budget with honest, up-front pricing. Print works hardest alongside the rest of your marketing — wraps, local SEO, paid ads and reviews — so the name people see around town is the name they search when they need you. Below are the questions trades owners ask us most about print.

How much does print marketing cost?

Design starts at $249 for a single piece or $899 for a full brand kit; printing and mailing are quoted per run based on quantity and stock. We give you real numbers up front and never mark up print behind your back.

Do you handle the printing and mailing too?

Yes. We design, print, finish, and can mail straight to the neighborhoods you want to win — including building the mailing list and targeting. You approve a proof; we handle the rest.

Does print still work for home-services businesses?

Absolutely. Direct mail routinely outperforms email response rates many times over, and a well-branded door hanger or yard sign keeps working in the neighborhood long after a digital ad disappears. Print is strongest paired with your website, wraps and reviews.

What areas do you serve?

We serve trades businesses across the Midwest — the Quad Cities, Peoria, Dubuque and surrounding communities in Illinois, Iowa, Missouri, Wisconsin and Indiana. If you're within about three hours of home, we can help.
