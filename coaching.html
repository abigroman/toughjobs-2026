What coaching covers

Every lever that moves a trades business.

No fluff, no whiteboard theory. We work on what actually pays the bills.

Business coaching for contractors and trades owners is the fastest way to turn a busy one-truck operation into a business that actually pays you what you're worth. If you're an electrician, plumber, HVAC tech, roofer, concrete contractor, painter, cleaner, or landscaper, you already know the trade cold. The part that keeps owners up at night isn't the work — it's the pricing, the hiring, the marketing, the paperwork, and the feeling that you're working harder every year for the same money. That's exactly the gap Toughjobs coaching closes.

Our trades business coaching is built for home-services companies, not corporate boardrooms. Every session is 1-on-1 with a coach who has actually built and scaled service businesses in the field. We don't hand you a binder of theory and disappear. We sit down with your real numbers, your real jobs, and your real market, then build a plan you can put to work the same week. Whether you need to raise your prices without losing customers, hire your first crew member, build systems so the business runs without you, or finally get your marketing to bring in the right jobs, coaching gives you a clear next step and someone to hold you to it.

Most owners try to figure all of this out alone, and it costs them years and tens of thousands of dollars in mistakes. A good contractor business coach shortcuts that curve. You get accountability that keeps you moving, honest answers when a decision matters, and a partner who wins when you win. Common questions

Trades business coaching FAQ.

What is business coaching for contractors?

It's 1-on-1 guidance focused on the business side of your trade — pricing, profit margins, hiring, systems, cash flow, and marketing. Instead of learning every lesson the hard way, you work with a coach who has already built service businesses and can point you straight at what will grow your revenue and buy back your time.

How much does a trades business coach cost?

Toughjobs coaching starts at $249 for a single 90-minute strategy session. Ongoing monthly coaching is $595 per month for two calls plus week-to-week accountability, and our hands-on Full Partner plan is $1,450 per month. Every plan is month-to-month with no long contracts.

Which trades do you coach?

We coach owners across the home-services trades — electricians, plumbers, HVAC, roofing, concrete and paving, painting, cleaning, landscaping, excavation, remodeling and general contracting. If you run a service business and want it to grow, coaching applies.

How fast will I see results?

Most owners walk away from the first session with pricing or workflow changes they can make immediately. Bigger wins — a hired crew, systems that run without you, a marketing engine that fills the schedule — typically take shape over the first 90 days of ongoing coaching.
