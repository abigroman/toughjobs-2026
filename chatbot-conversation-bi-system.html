01

What Information Should Be Saved

Every conversation is logged as a structured record — enough to score the lead, spot patterns, and follow up like a human would remember. Nothing more.

Visitor & Session Data

  • Timestamp, page URL where chat started
  • Trade / service category discussed
  • Referral source (Google, ad, direct, social)
  • Device type (mobile / desktop)
  • City/region (if volunteered or from ad targeting — not IP-tracked)

Conversation Content

  • Full question-and-answer transcript
  • Topics/tags matched (e.g. "pricing," "roofing," "reviews")
  • Questions the bot could NOT answer confidently
  • Whether the visitor was offered a human handoff, and if they accepted

Contact Info (only if given)

  • Name, phone, email, business name/trade — only if the visitor typed it in
  • Best time to call, project details volunteered

Outcome Data

  • Lead score assigned (see Section 3)
  • CTA clicked (if any)
  • Escalated to human? Y/N + reason
  • Follow-up status (contacted, booked, no response)
02

What Should NOT Be Saved

Excluded by Design

  • No payment or financial info — the bot never asks for or accepts card numbers, bank details, or SSNs.
  • No precise location tracking — no GPS coordinates or IP-based address lookups beyond general city/region.
  • No sensitive personal data — health info, family details, immigration status, or anything unrelated to the service inquiry.
  • No passwords or login credentials, even if a visitor accidentally pastes them.
  • No permanent raw chat logs tied to identity beyond the retention window (see Privacy Notice) — old anonymous conversation data is aggregated into insight reports, then the identifiable record is purged.
  • No third-party data sales — nothing collected here is ever sold or shared outside Toughjobs and the client business it was collected for.

04

High-Intent Trigger Rules

Certain phrases or behaviors trigger an immediate human alert, regardless of total score — these are moments where a live person closes the deal.

Instant Escalation Triggers

  • Visitor types phone number unprompted ("call me at...")
  • Emergency language: "leaking," "no heat," "urgent," "today," "ASAP"
  • Asks to speak to a real person / "can I talk to someone"
  • Explicitly asks for a quote/estimate on a specific project
  • Mentions a competitor by name and asks for comparison ("why you over X")
  • Expresses frustration or a complaint about a past experience

On trigger: bot sends a real-time SMS/Slack alert to the assigned sales contact with the transcript link, and tells the visitor "Someone from our team will reach out shortly" (never leaves them hanging).

07

Suggested FAQ Update Process

  1. Flag — any question the bot answers with low confidence, or that a human had to step in for, gets auto-tagged "FAQ gap."
  2. Batch weekly — gaps are collected into a short list every Friday.
  3. Draft — write a short + full answer in the approved format, matching tone (honest, caring, practical, never pushy).
  4. Review — a human (owner or marketing lead) approves before it goes live — no unreviewed answers reach the bot.
  5. Publish & monitor — new entry ships, and its usage is tracked for the next 2 weeks to confirm it's resolving the gap.

Privacy-Safe Chatbot Notice

Suggested On-Page Disclosure

"This chat is answered by an assistant to help you get quick answers about Toughjobs' services. We save the conversation and any contact info you choose to share so our team can follow up — we never sell your information, and we don't collect payment details or precise location through this chat. You can ask to speak with a real person anytime."

Retention

Identifiable conversation records are kept for 90 days to support follow-up, then anonymized into aggregate insight data (topics, scores, trends) with contact info removed.

11

Human Follow-Up Rules

High-Intent / Escalated

  • Alert sent in real time (SMS/Slack)
  • Human follow-up within 2 business hours
  • Phone call preferred over email for these

Medium Score

  • Added to nurture sequence (email/text)
  • Personal follow-up within 24–48 hours
  • No more than 2 follow-up touches without a reply

Low Score

  • No manual outreach — respects the visitor's low intent
  • May receive a single automated nurture email if opted in

Always

  • Never re-contact someone who explicitly said "just looking"
  • Every human follow-up references what was actually discussed — no generic scripts
