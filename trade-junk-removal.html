Junk Removal marketing FAQ

How much does junk removal marketing cost?

It depends on scope. A junk removal website is a one-time build with light monthly upkeep, while SEO and paid ads are ongoing monthly investments. We scope every junk removal marketing plan to your goals and market, and we give you real numbers up front — no surprise fees.

How fast will my junk removal business rank on Google?

Google Business Profile and on-page fixes usually move inside the first 30 days. Competitive rankings for terms like “junk removal near me” typically firm up over 60–90 days of steady work, with monthly reporting so you see exactly where your junk removal leads come from.

What areas do you serve?

We market junk removal companies across the Midwest — the Quad Cities, Peoria, Dubuque and the surrounding communities in Illinois, Iowa, Missouri, Wisconsin and Indiana. If you're within about three hours of home, we can serve your junk removal business.

Do I need a contract?

No long lock-in. We ask for about six months to get your junk removal marketing producing steady results, because SEO and ads compound over time — but we earn your business every month with real reporting, not fine print.
