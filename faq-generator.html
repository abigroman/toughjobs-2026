How to use this on your website

Where to place the FAQ

Drop the front-end HTML near the bottom of the page it belongs to — a service page, a city/location page, or a landing page that already targets this keyword. FAQs work best when they answer questions that page is actually about.

Two-step install

  1. Paste the Front-End HTML into your page body where you want the questions to appear.
  2. Paste the JSON-LD Schema into the page <head> or right before the closing </body> tag.

Connecting AI later

This tool generates questions from proven local-SEO templates, so it works instantly with no account. To swap in live AI writing later, a developer can replace the generateWithAI() placeholder in the page script with a call to your AI provider — the input fields and output formatting stay exactly the same.

Quality checklist before publishing

Read every answer out loud — it should sound like a real person, not a robot.
Confirm the visible questions match the schema questions exactly.
Remove any claim, price, or guarantee you can't back up.
Add real details — your hours, service area, and phone number.
Validate the schema in Google's Rich Results Test before going live.
Make sure the keyword reads naturally and isn't stuffed.
