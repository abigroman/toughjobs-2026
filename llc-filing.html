Illinois LLC filing assistance

Forming an LLC in Illinois.

Illinois LLCs are created by filing Articles of Organization with the Illinois Secretary of State. We prepare and submit that filing for you.

The numbers

What it costs

The Illinois state filing fee for Articles of Organization is $150. Our standard Illinois filing assistance is $195 total.

Need it faster? Illinois offers expedited processing for an extra $100 — an optional add-on that brings the total to $325. Expedited handling may require in-person filing and availability can vary, so we'll confirm before committing.

Illinois also requires an ongoing $75 annual report to stay in good standing — a separate, recurring state fee due each year after formation.

What we need from you

To get started

  • Your desired LLC name
  • Your principal business address in Illinois
  • A registered agent and address in Illinois
  • The names of the members / managers
  • Whether you want standard or expedited processing
Iowa LLC filing assistance

Forming an LLC in Iowa.

Iowa LLCs are created by filing a Certificate of Organization with the Iowa Secretary of State. We prepare and submit that filing for you.

The numbers

What it costs

The Iowa state filing fee for a Certificate of Organization is $50 — one of the lowest in the country. Our Iowa filing assistance is $65 total.

Iowa is unusual in that the state doesn't publish a standard form — the certificate has to meet the requirements of Iowa law. We handle that so your filing is accepted the first time.

Iowa also requires an ongoing $30 biennial report (filed online, every two years) to stay in good standing — a separate, recurring state fee.

What we need from you

To get started

  • Your desired LLC name
  • Your principal business mailing address
  • A registered agent and physical Iowa address
  • The names of the members / managers
  • Any effective date you want for the LLC

Questions, answered

LLC filing FAQ.

Is Toughjobs a law firm?

No. Toughjobs is not a law firm and does not provide legal, tax, or accounting advice. We provide administrative filing assistance — we help you organize your information, prepare your formation paperwork based on what you give us, and submit it to the state.

What does the price actually include?

Each price is your starting total for the initial LLC formation filing — Illinois is $195, Iowa is $65. Expedited Illinois processing is an optional add-on for an extra $100. We don't mark up the state's own fees.

How much does it cost to form an LLC in Illinois?

The Illinois state filing fee is $150, and our standard total is $195. Expedited Illinois processing is optional and adds a $100 fee, bringing the total to $325 if you choose it. Expedited handling may require in-person filing and availability can vary.

How much does it cost to form an LLC in Iowa?

The Iowa state filing fee is $50, and our total is $65. Iowa is one of the most affordable states in the country to form an LLC.

Are there ongoing fees after I file?

Yes. Illinois requires a $75 annual report fee, and Iowa requires a $30 biennial report fee (filed online every two years) to keep your LLC in good standing. These are separate, recurring state fees — not part of your initial filing total.

Why do prices say "starting"?

State fees are set by the state and can change at any time. We always confirm the current, final price with you before anything is filed, so there are no surprises.

How long does it take?

Timelines are set by the state, not by us. Iowa online filings are often approved within a business day or two; Illinois standard processing takes longer, and expedited is faster. We'll give you the current estimate when you start.

Do I need an attorney instead?

For a simple, straightforward LLC, most people don't. But if you have multiple owners with complex agreements, unusual tax questions, or legal risk, you should consult a licensed attorney or CPA — and we'll be honest with you if that's the better path.

Does forming an LLC protect me from lawsuits or save me on taxes?

We can't promise that. Forming an LLC does not guarantee liability protection, tax savings, loan approval, or business success. Those depend on how your business is run and your specific situation — questions for a licensed professional.

What's a registered agent, and do I need one?

A registered agent is a person or company with a physical address in your state who receives official mail for your LLC. Both Illinois and Iowa require one. You can often be your own, or we can help you coordinate a service as an add-on.

Can you also do my EIN, website, and branding?

Yes — those are optional add-ons priced separately. Once your LLC is filed, we can help with EIN setup, an operating agreement template, a website, logo and branding, a Google Business Profile, business email, and bookkeeping setup.

Who's responsible if my information is wrong?

You are. We file based on the details you provide, so it's important that your business name, addresses, and member information are accurate. We check your name against state records, but the underlying details are your responsibility.

Can you help if I'm not in Illinois or Iowa?

Right now our filing assistance covers Illinois and Iowa. If you're forming in another state, reach out and we'll point you in the right direction.
