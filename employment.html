Who we are

A marketing shop built
for the trades.

Toughjobs is a Central Illinois & Quad Cities marketing company that works exclusively with home-service and trade businesses — electricians, plumbers, roofers, HVAC techs, and the crews behind them. We're not a generalist agency chasing every industry. We know this world, we respect the people in it, and we build the websites, ad campaigns, vehicle wraps, and brands that help them grow.
