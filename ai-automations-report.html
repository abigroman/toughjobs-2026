01

Capture the Lead

Miss the first contact and the job is already your competitor's.

1

24/7 AI Voice Receptionist

A natural-sounding AI answers every inbound call in your business name, captures the problem, and books the job — even at 2 AM, on weekends, and while your crew is on the tools.

Profit impact: Home-services companies report a 30–50% lift in booked calls within 30 days. If you miss 30 calls/month at a 20% close rate and a $3,500 ticket, that's ~$21,000/month recovered against a ~$200/month tool — a 100×+ return.
Tools that do it
ToolNotes
Vapi / Retell AICustom voice agent, most flexible
Housecall Pro CSR AI, Jobber AI ReceptionistNative if you're already on them
NextPhone, Goodcall, DialzaraFlat-rate, fast setup
How to set it up
  1. Pull your last 30 days of calls; count how many went unanswered and multiply by average job value (your "opportunity number").
  2. Pick a tool; keep your existing number and forward unanswered calls to it.
  3. Feed it your services, pricing ranges, service area zips, and FAQs.
  4. Connect it to your scheduler (ServiceTitan / Housecall Pro / Jobber / Google Calendar) so it books directly.
  5. Set an escalation rule: complex or high-ticket calls forward to your cell.
Prompt to build the agent's script
# Paste into ChatGPT/Claude, then load the output into your voice tool You are the receptionist for [COMPANY], a [TRADE] company serving [CITIES]. Write a call-handling script that: greets in our name; asks for name, address, phone, and the problem; detects emergencies ([e.g. no heat, burst pipe, sparking panel]) and offers the soonest slot; quotes only these ranges [LIST]; books into [SCHEDULER]; and politely declines jobs outside these zips [LIST]. Keep it warm, plain-spoken, and under 3 sentences per turn. Output the opening line, 6 branch paths, and a confirmation summary.
2

Missed-Call Text-Back

The instant a call goes unanswered, an automatic SMS fires: "Sorry we missed you — reply here and we'll get right back." Keeps the lead warm instead of losing it to voicemail.

Profit impact: 85% of missed callers won't call back; automated text-back recovers up to 30% of otherwise-lost leads. Cheapest lead-recovery play there is.
Tools
ToolNotes
GoHighLevel, Podium, Emitrr, LeadTruffleText-back + lead qualification
Your FSM's built-in SMSHousecall Pro / Jobber
How to set it up
  1. Provision a dedicated SMS number or enable texting on your business line.
  2. Set the trigger: call status = missed → send SMS within 60 seconds.
  3. Write a benefit-driven first message (not a generic "sorry we missed you").
  4. Add TCPA-compliant opt-in language; route replies to a real inbox.
Prompt
Write 3 missed-call text-back messages for [COMPANY], a [TRADE] in [CITY]. Each under 320 characters, mentions we do free estimates + same-week appointments + are licensed/insured, ends with a question that gets a reply, and includes opt-in/STOP language. Friendly, human, no exclamation spam.
3

Speed-to-Lead Instant Callback

When a form is filled on your site or an ad (Google/Meta LSA), AI calls or texts the prospect within 60 seconds, qualifies them, and books.

Profit impact: Responding first wins ~78% of deals; after 5 minutes, contact rates fall off a cliff. Turns cold form-fills into booked jobs.
How to set it up
  1. Connect your web forms / lead sources (Angi, Thumbtack, LSA) into one inbox via Zapier or GoHighLevel.
  2. Trigger: new lead → AI dials/texts within 60 seconds.
  3. Qualify (project, timeline, address) then book or route hot leads to you.
Prompt
Draft a 60-second outbound call opener + 4 qualifying questions for a [TRADE] lead who just submitted a form for [SERVICE]. Goal: confirm the job, get the address, and book the soonest slot. Sound like a helpful human, not a robot.
4

Website Chat Widget That Books

An AI chat on your site answers pricing/scheduling/service-area questions 24/7 and captures the lead — even after hours when 25–35% of demand arrives.

Profit impact: Recovers after-hours web visitors who'd otherwise bounce to a competitor; every captured chat is a lead you already paid for with SEO/ads.
Tools
ToolNotes
Intercom / Tidio / your FSM chatGeneral widgets
GoHighLevel, Broadly, DaptaTrades-focused, books jobs
Prompt
Create a knowledge base + greeting for a website chatbot for [COMPANY] ([TRADE], [CITIES]). Include answers for: pricing ranges, service area, emergency availability, brands serviced, financing, and "how soon can you come out?" Always end by capturing name + phone + address and offering to book.
5

After-Hours & Overflow Agent

Route evenings, weekends, and overflow (when the line's busy) to AI so nothing goes to voicemail during the exact hours emergencies happen.

Profit impact: ~35–45% of home-services calls come outside business hours; closing that gap is pure found revenue with zero added payroll.
How to set it up
  1. In your phone system, forward calls to the AI after hours and on no-answer/busy.
  2. Give the after-hours agent a distinct script: emergencies → on-call cell; everything else → booked or callback queued for morning.
Prompt
Write an after-hours AI phone script for [TRADE] [COMPANY]. Triage: if it's an emergency ([LIST]), collect details and text the on-call tech immediately; otherwise book the first morning slot and send a confirmation text. Reassure the caller they're taken care of.
02

Book the Job

Turn a captured lead into a confirmed appointment with no phone tag.

6

Online Self-Scheduling

Customers pick their own slot from your real availability — the AI checks the calendar and writes the booking, no back-and-forth.

Profit impact: Fills the schedule while you sleep and cuts office phone time; booking is the #1 reason people call, so automating it removes your biggest bottleneck.
How to set it up
  1. Turn on online booking in your FSM (Housecall Pro / ServiceTitan / Jobber) or Calendly.
  2. Embed the booking link on your site, Google Business Profile, and text/email footers.
  3. Set buffer + travel time so slots stay realistic.
Prompt
Write the microcopy for an online booking flow for [TRADE]: service-type picker labels, a reassurance line about arrival windows, and a confirmation message with what to expect on the visit.
7

Emergency Detection & Triage

AI recognizes urgent keywords (no heat, burst pipe, sparks, water everywhere) and instantly bypasses normal booking to alert the on-call tech.

Profit impact: Emergency jobs are high-ticket and go to the first responder. Instant routing wins the $800 after-hours call instead of losing it.
Prompt
Give me an emergency-keyword list and triage rules for a [TRADE] business. For each emergency type, specify: urgency level, what to tell the customer, and who/what to alert. Keep non-emergencies in the normal booking flow.
8

Appointment Reminders & No-Show Recovery

Automatic SMS/email confirmations and reminders before the visit, plus an auto-rebook offer if someone cancels or no-shows.

Profit impact: Reminders measurably cut no-shows; each recovered slot is a full job saved and a truck-roll that doesn't get wasted.
Prompt
Write a reminder sequence for [TRADE] appointments: confirmation at booking, reminder 1 day before, reminder morning-of with arrival window, and a friendly rebook message if they miss it. SMS length, warm tone.
9

Instant Quote / Estimate Assistant

AI turns job details (or a photo + voice memo) into a ballpark range or a full estimate draft, so prospects get a number fast instead of waiting days.

Profit impact: AI estimators save 15–20+ hours/month and speed the quote — and the fastest quote often wins the job.
How to set it up
  1. Build a price book (services, materials, labor rates) the AI can reference.
  2. Use QuoteIQ / your FSM's estimate tool, or a custom GPT loaded with your pricing.
  3. Keep a human approval step before anything binding is sent.
Prompt
Act as an estimator for [COMPANY] ([TRADE]). Using this price book [PASTE], turn the following job description into a line-item estimate with a total range and a short scope summary. Flag anything that needs an on-site look. Job: [DESCRIPTION].

Follow Up

Most jobs are lost in the silence after the first quote. Automate the chase.

10

Unsold-Estimate Follow-Up

AI follows up on estimates that were sent but never accepted — reminders, financing options, and gentle urgency around seasonal pricing.

Profit impact: Converts 10–20% of previously-unsold estimates — revenue you already did the work to quote.
Prompt
Write a 3-touch follow-up sequence (day 2, day 5, day 12) for an unsold [TRADE] estimate of about $[AMOUNT] for [SERVICE]. Offer financing, answer the top 2 objections, and create honest seasonal urgency. SMS + email versions.
11

New-Lead Nurture Drip

Leads that don't book immediately get an automated, helpful sequence that keeps you top-of-mind through their decision.

Profit impact: Consistent follow-up is where contractors lose or win deals after the first call — automation makes it happen every time.
Prompt
Build a 5-message nurture drip for a [TRADE] lead who inquired but hasn't booked. Mix value (a tip, a trust-builder, a review highlight) with soft CTAs. Space over 10 days. Keep it human and local to [CITY].
12

Cold-Lead Re-Engagement

Leads that went quiet 30–90 days ago get an AI re-engagement touch across text/email to revive the ones still in-market.

Profit impact: Recaptures pipeline you'd otherwise write off; costs pennies per contact.
Prompt
Write a re-engagement message for [TRADE] leads who went cold 60+ days ago. One text + one email. Acknowledge the gap, add a reason to act now (seasonal need, limited slots), and make replying effortless.

Get Reviews

Your rating is the storefront. Automate the ask and the response.

13

Post-Job Review Request

A text asking for a Google review fires automatically within ~30 minutes of job completion — while satisfaction is highest and the tech is still fresh in mind.

Profit impact: SMS-within-30-min requests get a 25–40% response rate vs. 3–8% for later email — often 3–5× more reviews. A 20-job/day shop can generate 100–160 reviews/month, and the top Maps position captures 35–50% of local clicks.
How to set it up
  1. Trigger on job status = complete → send SMS after ~30 min.
  2. Include your direct Google review link (or QR).
  3. Optionally gate: happy customers → Google; unhappy → private feedback (see #15).
Prompt
Write 3 post-job review-request texts for [COMPANY] ([TRADE]). Reference the tech by name [VAR], thank them, make it one tap to leave a Google review, and stay under 300 characters. Warm, specific, no begging.
14

AI Review Responses

AI drafts on-brand replies to every review; only ~5% of businesses respond though 88% of consumers require it — easy way to stand out.

Profit impact: Businesses that reply to reviews average ~35% more revenue than those that don't; responding builds trust and local ranking.
Prompt
You reply to reviews for [COMPANY] ([TRADE]). Draft a warm, specific response to this review, in our voice, thanking them and inviting them back (or, if negative, taking it offline to [PHONE] and owning the fix). Review: [PASTE].
15

Bad-Review Interceptor

A quick post-job satisfaction check: happy customers are routed to Google; unhappy ones are routed to a private message to management first.

Profit impact: Catches the detractor before they post publicly — you get a window to fix it and often convert them into a loyal repeat customer.
Prompt
Design a 1-question SMS satisfaction check for [TRADE] jobs and the branching logic: 4–5 stars → Google review link; 1–3 stars → apologize, alert management at [EMAIL/PHONE], and promise a callback. Write all message variants.

Retain & Grow

The cheapest revenue is the customer you already earned.

16

Maintenance-Plan Renewal Automation

AI tracks agreement customers, schedules seasonal tune-ups, and sends renewal reminders before plans lapse.

Profit impact: Recovers 15–25% of memberships that would quietly lapse — the highest-ROI retention workflow in home services.
Prompt
Write a maintenance-plan renewal sequence for [TRADE]: reminder 30 days out, reminder at expiry, and a win-back 30 days after lapse. Emphasize priority service + savings. SMS + email.
17

Customer Win-Back Campaign

AI mines your CRM for customers not seen in 12–24 months and sends personalized, service-history-aware outreach with an incentive.

Profit impact: Recovers 8–15% of lapsed customers — revenue from people you already paid to acquire.
How to set it up
  1. Export "no contact in 12+ months" from your CRM.
  2. Segment by last service type; match a seasonal reason to reach out.
  3. Send a 2-message sequence with a loyalty offer; track bookings.
Prompt
Write a win-back message for a [TRADE] customer whose last service was [SERVICE] about [N] months ago. Personalize to that service, suggest the seasonally-relevant next step, include a returning-customer offer. Text + email.
18

Seasonal Service Reminders

Automatic reminders timed to the season and each customer's equipment/service (spring AC tune-up, fall furnace check, gutter season).

Profit impact: Smooths demand, fills slow weeks, and books recurring work without lifting a finger.
Prompt
Create a 12-month seasonal reminder calendar for [TRADE] in [REGION], with a short SMS for each seasonal service and the ideal send window.
19

Membership / Upsell on Every Job

AI (voice agent or follow-up) pitches the maintenance membership or a relevant add-on on every service interaction.

Profit impact: Recurring-plan attach rates rise to 25–40% (vs. 10–15% without) — the single biggest lever for turning one-off calls into recurring revenue.
Prompt
Write a natural membership pitch for [TRADE] to use at the end of every job: the one-line offer, the top 3 benefits, price framing, and a soft close. Give a version for the voice agent and one for a follow-up text.

Dispatch & Ops

More jobs per truck, per day, with less windshield time.

29

Smart Routing & Scheduling

AI sequences the day's jobs by location, priority, and real-time tech position to cut drive time and fit more calls in.

Profit impact: Fewer wasted miles = more billable jobs/day; GPS-fed routing typically returns 10–20× its cost within a quarter.
How to set it up
  1. Use route optimization inside ServiceTitan/Housecall Pro or a routing add-on.
  2. Add GPS tracking ($30–60/truck/month) so routing uses real locations.
  3. Let AI re-sequence when a job runs long/short.
Prompt
Given today's [N] jobs with these addresses, time windows, and priorities [PASTE], propose an optimized route order per tech that minimizes drive time and honors emergencies first. Explain the sequence briefly.
30

Predictive Inventory & Reorder

AI tracks truck stock and usage patterns, then flags or auto-generates reorders before a tech runs out mid-job.

Profit impact: Fewer return trips and no-parts delays; every avoided second trip is recovered labor and a happier customer.
Prompt
From this parts-usage history [PASTE], predict which items I'll need to reorder in the next 2 weeks for a [TRADE] operation with [N] trucks, and set sensible par levels for each.

By day 90, most home-services companies are booking 30–50% more jobs per month and converting 2–3× more one-off calls into recurring revenue — with the voice agent and membership pitch alone usually paying for the entire stack in week one.

Two guardrails before you flip anything on

  • Consent & compliance: outbound AI calls/texts need prior express written consent (TCPA); every text needs opt-in/STOP language. Reputable tools handle this — confirm it.
  • Data & licensing: ask any vendor where your customer data is stored, who can see it, and whether it's shared. And never let an AI commit to work outside your license scope.
