01

Main Chatbot Goals

The bot is a lead machine, not a help desk. Everything it does points at one outcome: get a qualified trade owner booked with Toughjobs.

GOAL 01

Qualify Fast

Figure out the trade, the market, and whether this is an owner with a budget — in the first few messages.

GOAL 02

Capture the Lead

Get name, business, trade, phone/email into the database. No conversation ends without a contact attempt.

GOAL 03

Score & Route

Rate every visitor hot / warm / cold and escalate hot ones to a human before they cool off.

GOAL 04

Educate & Build Trust

Answer real questions about pricing, timelines, and results in plain, no-BS language that matches our brand.

GOAL 05

Book the Call

Push warm/hot visitors to "Request a Quote" or the assessment. Booking a call is the win.

GOAL 06

Feed the Business

Log every conversation so we learn which trades, objections, and questions come up most — weekly.

05

Buyer Intent Stages

Where the visitor sits in the funnel decides the bot's tone and its ask.

STAGE 1

Unaware

Knows they need "more leads" but not that marketing is the fix. → Educate.

STAGE 2

Problem-Aware

Knows the problem (bad site, no calls). Exploring options. → Diagnose.

STAGE 3

Solution-Aware

Knows agencies exist, comparing. → Differentiate Toughjobs.

STAGE 4

Vendor-Aware

Considering Toughjobs specifically. Asking price/process. → Reassure & quote.

STAGE 5

Ready to Buy

Wants to talk now. → Book the call / escalate.

SIGNAL

Urgency Flags

"Slow season," "competitor beating me," "new truck" = push harder.

06

Lead Scoring Rules

Points-based. The bot adds/subtracts points as it learns about the visitor, then buckets them Hot / Warm / Cold.

SignalPointsNotes
Is a trade owner / decision-maker+30Core qualifier.
Trade is one we serve+15Any of the 27.
Inside service area (IL/IA/MO/WI/IN)+15Out-of-area = 0.
States a budget / asks pricing seriously+20Budget ≥ $1,000/mo = +10 more.
Wants leads "now" / urgent timeline+15Ready-to-buy signal.
Names a specific service or pain+10"My site's slow," "not ranking."
Gives phone or email+20Contactable.
Requests quote / assessment / call+25Strong conversion action.
Tire-kicker / no timeline−10Nurture, don't escalate.
Job seeker / vendor / out of area−30Disqualify.

Buckets

  • Hot  70+ points — escalate to a human immediately, capture contact, attempt live handoff or same-day callback.
  • Warm  40–69 points — capture contact, book a call, add to follow-up sequence.
  • Cold  Under 40 — educate, capture email if possible, drop into nurture.

08

Chatbot Escalation Rules

When the bot steps aside and gets a human involved.

  • Hot lead (70+): offer live handoff now; if none available, capture contact and promise same-day callback + alert the team.
  • Explicit ask: visitor says "talk to a person / call me / get me a human" → escalate regardless of score.
  • Quote / assessment requested: capture details, confirm, and route to sales.
  • Existing client with an issue: route to account/support, don't try to sell.
  • Complaint or frustration detected: apologize, escalate to a human fast.
  • Bot can't answer / repeated confusion: after 2 failed attempts, offer a human instead of guessing.
  • High-value trade + budget signal: restoration, solar, pools, GCs with $2K+ intent → prioritize handoff.
Handoff Rule

Never let a hot lead leave without a contact method captured. If a human isn't live, the bot books the next step — it never dead-ends.

09

Privacy Notice

Shown at the start of the chat and available on request.

  • State up front: "This chat is recorded to help us serve you and follow up."
  • Collect only what's needed to qualify and contact — name, business, trade, location, contact info.
  • Explain use: to answer questions, prepare a quote, and follow up about Toughjobs services. No selling data to third parties.
  • Consent for contact: capturing a phone/email = permission for Toughjobs to reach out about their inquiry.
  • Link to the full privacy policy; offer opt-out / data-deletion on request.
  • Don't request sensitive data (SSNs, payment card numbers) in chat.

Legal to review final wording before launch. Bot should never promise confidentiality it can't keep.
